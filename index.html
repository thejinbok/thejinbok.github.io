About

Around the age of 6, I started to become interested in chess when I saw my schoolmates playing. I asked them to teach me, but nobody wanted to, so in a week, just watching them play, I learned the basic moves. For almost 3 years I didn't win a game, but I kept playing until I got my first victory against my neighbor. Since then I became almost unstoppable, I even became a four-time champion in four consecutive years of the regional chess championship.

My chess story illustrates very well who I am. At the beginning I tend to have a lot of difficulty, but I adapt and don't give up. Consequently, as time goes on in this learning cycle, I achieve mastery.

My name is Jin Bok. I'm a pro gamer, skateboarder, software engineer, investor, and businessman, currently living in São Paulo, Brazil.
